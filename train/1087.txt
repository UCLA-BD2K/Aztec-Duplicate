

	This  WSDL file describe the phospho.ELM database Web Service. The phospho.ELM database contains descriptions of sets of one or more orthologous proteins that are annotated to be phosphorylated.
	The orthology grouping allows convenient cross-species comparison.
	
	Reference:
	Phospho.ELM: A database of experimentally verified phosphorylation sites in eukaryotic proteins
    Francesca Diella, Scott Cameron, Christine Gemuend, Rune Linding, Allegra Via, Bernhard Kuster, Thomas Sicheritz-Ponten, 
    Nikolaj Blom  and Toby J Gibson  (2004) BMC Bioinformatics, 5:79
	
	URL:http://phospho.elm.eu.org
