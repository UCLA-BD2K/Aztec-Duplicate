iBrain™ is an easy-to-use neuroimage processing and visualisation package developed by David Abbott and his team. The software is free for non-commercial use. From its roots in 1995 as an in-house software platform for functional MRI research, iBrain™ has grown to accommodate many requirements of neuroimaging analysis. iBrain™ can be used stand-alone, or in conjunction with other analysis packages such as SPM
