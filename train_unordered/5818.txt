ORS Viewer Lite is an advanced image viewing and analysis workstation approved by the US FDA, Health Canada, and CE marked for Europe. Extensive configuration options are available, including custom rules sets for study anonymization. A web interface is available for quick configuration of DICOM networking. ORS Viewer integrates into the clinical workflow with DICOM networking for query/retrieve, user configuration and login control, image fusion and registration, modality-specific specialized modes, and DICOM print, report, and media creation. A commercial version is available which adds 3D viewing, image segmentation, specialty imaging plugins, and integration with an available web and mobile viewing add-on.
