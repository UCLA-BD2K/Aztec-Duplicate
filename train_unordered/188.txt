Enables querying of KEGG databases (pathway, ligand, disease, gene, drug etc). 
Use general form of URL http://rest.kegg.jp/{operation}/{argument}/{argument2 or option}
where operation is one of info, list, find, get, conv, link
arguments are  databases or database entries 

Try http://rest.kegg.jp/{operation}/{database}/{term}
operation = list, database = pathway and term = hsa
